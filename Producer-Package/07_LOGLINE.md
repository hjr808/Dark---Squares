# LOGLINE

> A young man growing up between loyalty, ambition, and survival discovers that every
> choice casts a shadow — and some shadows become dark squares that change lives forever.

---

## Series Logline

**DARK SQUARES** follows Manny, a disciplined and protective older brother, and his
younger brother Keith, a naturally gifted freestyler, as they grow from childhood
in the Woods into a world of music, basketball, street money, and consequence —
culminating in a single birthday night that becomes the event their neighborhood
will call **Bloody Sweet 16** forever.

---

## Season One Logline

In Season One, Manny and Keith navigate a childhood defined by their mother's strict
house rules, a neighborhood that tests everything, and the slow formation of a circle
bound by music, loyalty, and the specific trust of people who have seen each other
in bad moments. When Manny's Sweet Sixteen party becomes the scene of an irreversible
act of violence, the birthday ends — and the consequences begin.
