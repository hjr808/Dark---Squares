# CREATOR BIOGRAPHY

## Alfred HJR

Alfred HJR is the creator, writer, and executive producer of *Dark Squares*, a premium
one-hour serialized television drama. The series draws on his personal knowledge of
urban street culture, the specific geography and social rules of inner-city childhood,
hip-hop and turntable culture, and the emotional terrain of brotherhood under pressure.

Alfred HJR has developed *Dark Squares* as a complete creative project, including a
twenty-one-episode Season One source narrative, a series bible, character profiles,
an episode guide, and Season Two planning documents — all assembled with the discipline
and long-range vision of a creator building a franchise rather than pitching a premise.

The authenticity of *Dark Squares* comes from the creator's direct relationship with
the world the series depicts. The music is not decorative. The street language is not
approximated. The moral complexity of the brother relationship at the center of the
series is not borrowed from other shows. It is built from the specific knowledge of
what it means to grow up between loyalty and ambition and to live with the permanent
weight of the choices made in those years.

Alfred HJR retains full creative and intellectual-property ownership of *Dark Squares*.
He is actively seeking a production partner, representation, or development deal with
the right collaborator who understands what the series already is and is committed
to bringing it to screen with integrity.
