### Notes on Source Integrity

All narrative facts in this producer package are sourced from the canonical materials
listed above, primarily `Book-1/Dark_Squares_Book_One_Season_One_Manuscript.docx`
and `Series-Bible/MASTER_CANON.md`. No story material has been invented or
extrapolated beyond what those sources establish.
