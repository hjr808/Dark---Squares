# DARK SQUARES
## Episode 2 — "MONDAY"

**Television Screenplay Adaptation**

---

## TEASER

### INT. ELLA MAE'S APARTMENT — DAY

A hard KNOCK hits the apartment door.

Keith looks up first. Manny freezes. Kizzy lifts her head and gives one sharp BARK.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Monday started like a regular day until somebody knocked on the apartment door.

Another KNOCK.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Not a polite knock. A testing knock.

Keith edges toward the window. Manny checks the clock.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Their mother would be home in less than thirty minutes.

Kizzy growls at the door.

**CUT TO TITLE: DARK SQUARES**

---

## ACT ONE

### INT. ELLA MAE'S APARTMENT — CONTINUOUS

The KNOCK comes again.

**VOICE (O.S.)**
We know y'all in there.

Keith looks to Manny.

**KEITH**
(whispering)
Don't open it.

Manny takes a bat from beside the couch. He does not raise it. He holds it low.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
The boys at the door were not there for homework. They were not there to play. They had followed Keith downstairs after some words upstairs.

Manny looks at the clock again.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
If Ella Mae walked in and found trouble at her door, the punishment would last longer than any fight.

Manny moves to the door. Keith stays back with Kizzy.

### INT. APARTMENT HALLWAY / ELLA MAE'S APARTMENT — CONTINUOUS

Manny cracks the door just enough to be seen, not enough to let anybody in.

Three BOYS stand in the hallway. One has his hands in his pockets. One leans forward, waiting for Manny to swing first. The third keeps checking the stairwell.

Manny does not raise his voice.

**MANNY**
Go home.

One boy laughs.

**BOY #1**
We ain't scared of you.

**MANNY**
I didn't ask if you was scared. I said go home.

Nobody moves.

---

## ACT TWO

### INT. APARTMENT HALLWAY — CONTINUOUS

The outside back door OPENS somewhere behind the building.

Manny hears it. The three boys hear it too.

Footsteps approach.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Ella Mae.

The boys' posture changes at once. One backs toward the stairs. The other two follow. They vanish upstairs just as the footsteps reach the hall.

Manny shuts the door.

### INT. ELLA MAE'S APARTMENT — CONTINUOUS

Manny slides the bat behind the couch. Keith drops onto the couch, trying to look normal. Kizzy paces in a circle, still suspicious.

The apartment door opens. Ella Mae enters with her work bag.

She looks from Manny to Keith.

**ELLA MAE**
Why y'all so quiet?

Manny shrugs.

**MANNY**
We good.

Keith nods too fast.

**KEITH**
Yeah.

Ella Mae looks toward the ceiling, where running feet fade away. She knows something happened. She also knows it did not make it into her house.

She keeps it smooth.

**ELLA MAE**
Wash your hands. Dinner almost ready.

Manny and Keith exchange a look.

---

## TAG

### INT. ELLA MAE'S APARTMENT — LATER

The apartment is calm. Dinner dishes sit in the sink. Kizzy sleeps on the floor.

Manny and Keith sit close enough to whisper.

They finally laugh.

The laugh is nervous.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
They had been close. Too close.

**FADE OUT.**

**END OF EPISODE**
