# DARK SQUARES
## Episode 4 — "THE GRAVEYARD BET"

**Television Screenplay Adaptation**

---

## TEASER

### EXT. THE WOODS — LATE AFTERNOON

Manny, Keith, and Kizzy linger on the block. An unseen kid calls out from a porch.

**KID (O.S.)**
Keith scared.

Keith turns, insulted.

**KEITH**
I ain't scared.

Manny smirks.

**MANNY**
Everybody scared of something.

Keith turns on him.

**KEITH**
What you scared of?

**MANNY**
Not the graveyard.

Keith holds Manny's gaze.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
The bet started over nothing. Most dangerous ideas did.

**CUT TO TITLE: DARK SQUARES**

---

## ACT ONE

### EXT. GRAVEYARD GATE — NIGHT

Night settles over the Woods. Manny and Keith stand at the gate, trying not to show what they feel. Kizzy trots between them, alert and proud.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
By the time night settled over the Woods, the bet had become official. Whoever backed out had to admit the other one was braver.

Keith looks at Manny.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Keith would rather walk through fire than admit that.

Manny pushes the gate open. It CREAKS.

### EXT. GRAVEYARD — CONTINUOUS

Grass. Headstones. Trees bent by the wind. The boys step in with Kizzy.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
During the day, it was grass and stones and quiet. At night, it became something else.

Wind moves low over the ground. Every shadow seems to have a reason.

Keith leans close to Manny.

**KEITH**
(whispering)
This was your idea.

**MANNY**
It was your mouth.

**KEITH**
You older.

**MANNY**
You bet.

---

## ACT TWO

### EXT. GRAVEYARD — CONTINUOUS

Kizzy suddenly stops.

Manny stops. Keith stops.

**KEITH**
(whispering)
Why she stop?

Manny swallows.

**MANNY**
Because you stopped.

**KEITH**
I stopped because she stopped.

Kizzy growls into the dark between two headstones.

That is enough.

Keith RUNS.

Manny runs after him.

Kizzy barks once, then runs past both of them like she has been waiting for the race to start.

### EXT. THE WOODS STREET — MOMENTS LATER

The brothers burst out to the street and stop. Keith bends over, laughing and gasping.

**KEITH**
You ran first!

**MANNY**
You ran because I moved.

**KEITH**
No, you ran.

**MANNY**
Kizzy ran first.

They both look at Kizzy. She stands in the street, tail wagging, proud of herself.

---

## TAG

### EXT. CURB — NIGHT

Manny and Keith collapse onto the curb, laughing until they cannot breathe. Kizzy sits in front of them.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
The graveyard bet became one of those stories Keith told differently every time. In his version, Manny screamed. In Manny's version, Keith almost cried.

Kizzy pants happily.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
In Kizzy's version, if she could have told it, she saved them both.

The boys laugh again.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
The truth did not matter. The memory did.

**FADE OUT.**

**END OF EPISODE**
