# DARK SQUARES
## Episode 5 — "STEVE'S TURNTABLES"

**Television Screenplay Adaptation**

---

## TEASER

### INT. STEVE'S ROOM — DAY

Manny stands in Steve's doorway. He takes in the room: records against the wall, speakers in the corners, wires across the floor, and two real turntables.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Steve lived across town and had something Manny had only seen from a distance.

Manny looks at the turntables.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Real turntables.

Steve smiles at Manny's awe.

**STEVE**
You ever blend records?

Manny shakes his head.

**STEVE**
You wanna learn?

Manny nods before he can pretend to be cool.

**CUT TO TITLE: DARK SQUARES**

---

## ACT ONE

### INT. STEVE'S ROOM — CONTINUOUS

Steve places Manny's hands near the equipment with care.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
The first Saturday Manny walked into Steve's room, he stopped at the door like he had entered a church.

Steve cues a record, then another.

**STEVE**
Listen for the beat.

Manny listens.

**STEVE**
Don't count like you counting. Feel when one song ready to meet the other.

Manny tries. He comes in too early. The records clash.

He tries again. Too late.

Again. Too fast.

Manny pulls his hand back, embarrassed.

Steve does not laugh.

**STEVE**
Again.

Manny nods and tries again.

### INT. STEVE'S ROOM — SERIES OF SATURDAYS

— Manny practices, unsure.

— Steve watches, patient.

— Manny misses a blend, resets, tries again.

— On the fourth Saturday, Manny catches the space between songs. The transition lands.

Steve gives a small nod. Manny hears it too.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
The mistakes were still there, but something else had arrived. Instinct.

---

## ACT TWO

### EXT. BASKETBALL COURT — DAY

Manny crosses over an opponent, sees an opening, and passes before the defender knows it is coming.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Music was not just sound. It was timing. It was patience. It was control.

The ball hits the net.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Basketball had rhythm too. A crossover. A pass. A shot released before the defender knew it was coming.

Manny gathers the ball, thinking about the turntables.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Music and basketball were cousins. Manny could feel that before he could explain it.

### INT. ELLA MAE'S APARTMENT — AFTERNOON

Manny's practice tape sits in his room. Keith slips in, finds it, and presses PLAY.

A beat comes through the speaker.

Keith talks over it, playing at first. Then he rhymes. Then he freestyles—easy, unforced, like he has been waiting for this beat.

Manny appears in the doorway. He does not interrupt.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Manny kept that world separate. Steve was Manny's place. The records were Manny's secret.

Keith keeps going. Manny listens, stunned and proud.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
But secrets inside small houses never stay hidden forever.

Keith finally looks up, embarrassed to find Manny there.

Manny says nothing. He lets Keith have the moment.

---

## TAG

### INT. ELLA MAE'S APARTMENT — CONTINUOUS

The tape plays. Keith looks at Manny. Manny looks back, knowing.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Manny had discovered music.

Keith turns back to the beat.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
But Keith had discovered his voice.

**FADE OUT.**

**END OF EPISODE**
