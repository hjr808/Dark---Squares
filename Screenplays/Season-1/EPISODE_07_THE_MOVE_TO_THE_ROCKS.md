# DARK SQUARES
## Episode 7 — "THE MOVE TO THE ROCKS"

**Television Screenplay Adaptation**

---

## TEASER

**FADE IN:**

### INT. ELLA MAE'S APARTMENT — DAY

Ella Mae stands at the table. Manny looks up. Keith stops chewing.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Ella Mae did not announce the move like a celebration. She said it like a decision.

**ELLA MAE**
We found a house.

Manny and Keith absorb it.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
A house meant something different to a family that had lived in an apartment where footsteps overhead could wake you up and hallway drama could arrive at your door before dinner.

**CUT TO TITLE: DARK SQUARES**

---

## ACT ONE

### INT. ELLA MAE'S APARTMENT — CONTINUOUS

Manny sees the house in pieces as Ella Mae speaks.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
A house meant space. A basement. A garage. A fence. A yard. A front door that belonged to them.

Keith smiles at the idea. Manny does not know what to feel.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
It also meant leaving the Woods.

### EXT. THE WOODS — DAY

Manny walks past the junkyard, the graveyard gate, the building, porches, and the streetlights. Keith and Kizzy race ahead.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
He wanted better. He wanted quiet. He wanted his mother to have something that looked like progress. But the Woods had raised them.

The brothers pass the corners that hold their memories.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
You can move out of a neighborhood. But you do not move out of what it made you.

---

## ACT TWO

### EXT. ELLA MAE'S HOUSE, THE ROCKS — DAY

Moving boxes. Cleaner streets. Bigger houses. More driveways. The new house has a garage, yard, fence, porch, and basement door.

People on porches watch Manny and Keith unload.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
The Rocks felt different immediately. People still watched from porches, but they watched differently. Like they were measuring.

Keith clocks them first.

**KEITH**
They looking at us.

Manny nods.

**MANNY**
Let them look.

**KEITH**
You think they tough?

Manny looks down the street.

**MANNY**
Everybody tough till they meet somebody from the Woods.

Keith laughs. The laugh helps.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Still, the Rocks had its own rules. Every neighborhood did.

Manny and Keith carry another box inside.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
There were boys who felt they controlled the block, boys who tested newcomers just to see if they could be moved.

---

## TAG

### EXT. ELLA MAE'S HOUSE, THE ROCKS — DUSK

The family settles into the new house. Manny stands at the porch. Keith shoots at a hoop above the garage. Kizzy runs the fence line.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Ella Mae's house became the new center of their world. The garage became a basketball court. The basement became a place for birthdays, food, and noise. The fence became a boundary. The porch became a lookout.

Manny watches the new block watching back.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
It was better. It was not innocent. Nothing ever was.

**FADE OUT.**

**END OF EPISODE**
