# DARK SQUARES
## Episode 8 — "THE TEST"

**Television Screenplay Adaptation**

---

## TEASER

**FADE IN:**

### EXT. THE ROCKS — DAY

Manny and Keith walk past a group of neighborhood boys. A comment follows them. Later, a shove lands during a game. The tests come slow.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Every new neighborhood had a welcoming committee. Not official. Not friendly. Just boys who wanted to know what you were made of.

A BOY steps in front of Manny and Keith.

**BOY**
Where y'all from?

Manny understands the real question.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Who are you? What do you know? Can we push you?

**CUT TO TITLE: DARK SQUARES**

---

## ACT ONE

### EXT. THE ROCKS — CONTINUOUS

Keith watches Manny answer.

**MANNY**
The Woods.

The air changes. A few boys look at each other.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
The Woods had a reputation, and reputations traveled faster than people.

Manny and Keith walk on. Nobody follows.

### EXT. BACKYARD BASKETBALL COURT — AFTERNOON

A rough game. Hard fouls. Talking. Elbows. One boy bumps Keith after the whistle.

Keith squares up. Manny steps between them.

**MANNY**
Play ball.

The boy smirks.

**BOY**
I am.

**MANNY**
Then play.

Nobody swings. Nobody has to.

---

## ACT TWO

### EXT. BACKYARD BASKETBALL COURT — CONTINUOUS

The ball is checked back in. The game resumes.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
The message had been delivered. The Rocks learned something that day. Manny and Keith were not trying to take over anything, but they were not easy prey.

### EXT. ROCKS DRIVEWAYS — SERIES OF DAYS

— Manny plays street ball beneath hoops over garage doors.

— He uses angles to get around bigger players.

— He finds space where they thought there was none.

— Keith watches the crowd gather around his brother.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
After that, things got smoother. Not perfect. Smoother.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Basketball gave him respect in the Rocks. Music gave him somewhere else to go.

---

## TAG

### EXT. DRIVEWAY — DUSK

Manny dribbles alone as the daylight fades. Keith watches from the curb, then looks toward the voices gathering down the block.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Between the court, Steve's turntables, and the memory of the Woods, Manny was becoming two things at once. Athlete and producer. Big brother and dreamer. Son of a working mother and student of the streets.

Keith heads toward the voices. Manny keeps working the ball.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Keith wanted the crowd. Manny wanted the craft. That difference would matter later.

**FADE OUT.**

**END OF EPISODE**
