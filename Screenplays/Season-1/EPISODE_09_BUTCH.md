# DARK SQUARES
## Episode 9 — "BUTCH"

**Television Screenplay Adaptation**

---

## TEASER

**FADE IN:**

### EXT. ROCKS DRIVEWAY — DAY

Manny shoots alone. **BUTCH**, dark-skinned, calm, sharp-eyed, watches from the sidewalk. He does not try to impress anybody.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Manny met Butch in the Rocks even though Butch belonged to the Woods.

Manny sees him watching.

**MANNY**
You play?

**BUTCH**
Enough.

**MANNY**
That mean you good or you scared?

Butch smiles.

**BUTCH**
Check ball.

**CUT TO TITLE: DARK SQUARES**

---

## ACT ONE

### EXT. ROCKS DRIVEWAY — CONTINUOUS

The game starts. Manny plays with rhythm and quickness; Butch with strength and patience.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Some friendships begin with long conversations. Theirs began with a basketball.

The game goes on through hard shots, hard fouls, arguments, and laughter. Neither gives an inch.

The light changes. They stop, both breathing hard.

They do not say they respect one another. They do not have to.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
By the end of the game, neither boy had to say they respected the other. The respect was already there.

### EXT. ROCKS STREET — LATER

Keith walks with Manny after the game.

**KEITH**
He cool.

**MANNY**
You say that about everybody.

**KEITH**
Nah. He different.

Manny considers that.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Keith was right.

---

## ACT TWO

### EXT. ROCKS DRIVEWAY — ANOTHER DAY

Butch sits with a guitar in his hands. He works through a chord, listening closely. Manny listens too.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Butch loved music too. Not just rap. Guitar. Blues. Rock. Soul. He had a way of hearing sadness inside a chord.

Butch lets the chord ring out.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Years later, that would become one of the parts of him people forgot to mention when they talked about the streets.

### EXT. ROCKS STREET — CONTINUOUS

Butch watches people move along the block. Manny watches Butch watching.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
The streets were there too. Even young, Butch understood money differently. While other boys thought about spending, Butch thought about movement.

Butch and Manny exchange a quiet look of understanding.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
How something became more. How a connection became opportunity. How loyalty became a network.

---

## TAG

### EXT. ROCKS DRIVEWAY — DUSK

Manny and Butch shoot around. Keith retrieves the ball and throws it back into play. Kizzy crosses between them.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Manny saw that and respected it. Butch saw Manny's discipline and respected that.

Butch checks the ball to Manny. Manny drives. Butch guards.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
They were not brothers by blood. But life does not always care about blood. Sometimes it gives you a person and lets time decide the rest.

**FADE OUT.**

**END OF EPISODE**
