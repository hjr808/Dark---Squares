# DARK SQUARES
## Episode 10 — "LUCKY'S ROOM"

**Television Screenplay Adaptation**

---

## TEASER

**FADE IN:**

### INT. LUCKY'S ROOM / STUDIO — DAY

Equipment is squeezed into corners. Wires go wherever they fit. A microphone waits. Speakers make the room sound bigger than it is.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Lucky's studio was not really a studio. Not at first.

Steve brings Manny through the door to **LUCKY**, the same age but carrying himself like an engineer, producer, mechanic, and dreamer rolled into one.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Steve had been the doorway. Lucky became the room.

Lucky adjusts a cable. Sound clears from the speakers.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
To them, it was everything.

**CUT TO TITLE: DARK SQUARES**

---

## ACT ONE

### INT. LUCKY'S ROOM / STUDIO — CONTINUOUS

Lucky works without complaint: fixes a loose connection, changes a cable, adjusts a level, listens for what is wrong until he finds it.

Manny watches, absorbed.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
If something broke, he fixed it. If a cable went bad, he found another. If the sound was wrong, he did not complain. He listened until he found the problem.

Lucky brings Manny to the mixer, the levels, and the record crates.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Manny learned the mixer. The levels. The record crates. The difference between hearing a song and understanding it.

Manny listens to the track in a new way.

### INT. LUCKY'S ROOM / STUDIO — LATER

The room has more traffic now: friends of friends, boys with talent, boys who want to hear themselves on tape, boys who want to see what is inside.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
People came through houses when music started moving. Strangers. Friends of friends. Boys with talent. Boys with envy.

Lucky watches the door before shutting it.

---

## ACT TWO

### INT. LUCKY'S ROOM / STUDIO — AFTERNOON

Lucky opens a drawer. Manny sees a gun inside and freezes.

Lucky notices.

**LUCKY**
Don't touch it unless you have to.

Manny nods. Lucky lets him hold it for the first time. Manny feels its weight, then Lucky puts it away.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
The weight stayed in his hand even after Lucky put it away.

Manny looks from the drawer to the microphones.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
That was the studio too. Dreams and danger in the same room. Microphones and protection. Music and reality.

### INT. LUCKY'S ROOM / STUDIO — LATER

Keith stands near the mic. A beat comes on.

**VOICE (O.S.)**
Say something.

**KEITH**
I got nothing.

Then Keith starts freestyling. The room changes.

Lucky looks at Manny. Manny looks back.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Both of them knew. Keith had something.

---

## TAG

### INT. LUCKY'S ROOM / STUDIO — NIGHT

Manny, Keith, Lucky, and Butch fill the small room. Sometimes Cheerio is there. Sometimes other voices drift through. The equipment hums; the beat continues.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
The studio became a second home after that. Everybody chasing something.

Manny watches Keith at the mic, then Lucky at the board.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Nobody knew yet how expensive dreams could become.

**FADE OUT.**

**END OF EPISODE**
