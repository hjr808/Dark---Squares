# DARK SQUARES
## Episode 12 — "THE MAX JULIAN"

**Television Screenplay Adaptation**

---

## TEASER

**FADE IN:**

### EXT. BUS STOP — WINTER DAY

Winter presses down on the city. Faces hurt in the cold. Hands disappear into sleeves.

Manny waits in a Max Julian winter coat, fur collar pulled close. A WOMAN, a CHILD, and a BOY Manny does not know wait nearby. Nobody talks.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Winter hit the city like punishment. The kind of cold that made faces hurt and hands disappear into sleeves.

Manny touches the collar.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Ella Mae had worked hard for that coat. It was not just warmth. It was pride.

Two OLDER BOYS cross the street. They do not hurry.

**CUT TO TITLE: DARK SQUARES**

---

## ACT ONE

### EXT. BUS STOP — CONTINUOUS

The older boys approach Manny. One looks at the coat and smiles.

**OLDER BOY #1**
Nice Max Julian.

Manny knows. Still, part of him hopes he is wrong.

The second boy steps close.

**OLDER BOY #2**
Take it off.

Manny stares at him.

**MANNY**
What?

The boy lifts his sweatshirt just enough to show the handle tucked in his waistband.

No speech. No performance. Just enough.

Manny unzips the coat. His fingers feel far away from his body. He slides it from his shoulders and hands it over.

The cold hits him immediately.

The older boys back away with the coat and disappear around the corner.

---

## ACT TWO

### INT. CITY BUS — LATER

The bus arrives late. Nobody speaks.

Manny sits without his coat. The unknown boy sits a few rows away, watching but not pressing.

### INT. CITY BUS — THE WOODS STOP — LATER

The bus reaches the Woods. The boy stands and looks back at Manny.

**NATE**
You alright?

Manny nods once.

**MANNY**
Yeah.

The boy nods.

**NATE**
My name Nate.

**MANNY**
Manny.

That is all. No friendship. No promise.

---

## TAG

### EXT. THE WOODS BUS STOP — WINTER DAY

Manny steps off the bus into the cold. Nate watches from the bus door before it pulls away.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Just two boys connected by a cold afternoon and something neither of them would forget.

The bus carries Nate away.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Years later, the neighborhood would know him as Nutty Nate. But that day, he was just Nate. A witness.

Manny walks home without the Max Julian.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Sometimes that is how important people enter a life. Quietly.

**FADE OUT.**

**END OF EPISODE**
