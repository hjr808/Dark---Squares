# DARK SQUARES
## Episode 13 — "CONEY NIGHTS"

**Television Screenplay Adaptation**

---

## TEASER

**FADE IN:**

### INT. CONEY ISLAND RESTAURANT — NIGHT

Neon in the window. Grease on the grill. Fries drop into hot oil. Booths are full of kids staying too long because nobody wants to go home yet.

Manny, Butch, Lucky, and Keith slide into a booth like they own it.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
The Coney Island was bright at night.

Fries are shared. Pop is stretched. Conversation is free.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
They did not have much money, but Coney Island made a few dollars feel like enough.

**CUT TO TITLE: DARK SQUARES**

---

## ACT ONE

### EXT. CONEY ISLAND PARKING LOT — NIGHT

Dice click against concrete. A loose circle forms near the parking lot. Five dollars. Ten dollars. Mostly talking.

Butch laughs and steps into the circle. Manny follows. Lucky stays close enough to watch and far enough to claim he is not involved. Keith floats between everybody, making jokes.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Nobody got rich. Nobody cared.

The dice keep clicking. Money changes hands. The group laughs and heads back inside.

### INT. CONEY ISLAND RESTAURANT — NIGHT

The doorbell rings above the entrance.

Manny looks up. **NATE** stands near the counter: the boy from the bus stop.

Their eyes meet. Nate nods. Manny nods back.

This time Nate walks over.

**NATE**
Mind if I sit?

Butch slides over.

**BUTCH**
Pull up.

---

## ACT TWO

### INT. CONEY ISLAND RESTAURANT — CONTINUOUS

Nate sits with his food and looks at Manny.

**NATE**
You probably don't remember me.

Manny smiles.

**MANNY**
I remember.

**NATE**
The bus stop.

**MANNY**
Yeah.

The table grows quiet. Nobody asks. Nobody jokes. Some stories do not need to be retold in public.

Keith studies his fries, then breaks the quiet.

**KEITH**
These fries shorter than my future.

The booth explodes with laughter.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Just like that, Nate was in. Not officially. Nothing in the neighborhood was official.

---

## TAG

### EXT. CONEY ISLAND / STREET TO LUCKY'S STUDIO — NIGHT

The five boys leave Coney Island together and walk toward Lucky's studio.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
One day it had been four friends. The next, it was five.

Nate walks with them, no longer outside the circle.

**FADE OUT.**

**END OF EPISODE**
