# DARK SQUARES
## Episode 14 — "FIRST DAY"

**Television Screenplay Adaptation**

---

## TEASER

**FADE IN:**

### INT. HIGH SCHOOL — DAY

Big hallways. Bigger crowds. Bigger voices. Lockers run in both directions. Students move like traffic.

Manny enters and pauses for half a second.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
High school made everything bigger. Bigger chances to disappear.

Freshmen try to look like they know where they are going. Seniors move like the building belongs to them.

Butch finds Manny at the lockers.

**BUTCH**
You lost?

**MANNY**
I was.

**BUTCH**
You still are.

Manny laughs.

**CUT TO TITLE: DARK SQUARES**

---

## ACT ONE

### INT. HIGH SCHOOL HALLWAY — CONTINUOUS

The warning bell rings, but Manny and Butch do not move fast. The hallway changes—not silent, just aware.

**SARAH** walks toward the lockers with books against her chest. Light brown hair. Green eyes. Calm posture. She is not trying to get attention, which makes people give it anyway.

She stops across from Manny and Butch.

**SARAH**
Excuse me.

Manny and Butch step aside.

**SARAH**
Thank you.

Her voice is proper without being stiff. Soft without being weak.

Sarah walks on. Butch looks at Manny.

**BUTCH**
Who that?

**MANNY**
I don't know.

**BUTCH**
You gonna find out?

**MANNY**
You are.

Butch grins.

**BUTCH**
Maybe.

---

## ACT TWO

### INT. HIGH SCHOOL HALLWAY — LATER

Butch introduces himself to Sarah first. She receives him with careful politeness, watching before she decides.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Her name was Sarah. She met Butch first at the lockers. That mattered later.

Manny watches more than he speaks.

### INT. HIGH SCHOOL — SERIES OF DAYS

— Locker combinations.

— Lunch tables.

— Hallway jokes.

— Basketball practice.

— Sarah learning the crew. The crew learning Sarah.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
The friendship grew in small pieces.

Sarah listens as the others talk about the Woods and the Rocks. She does not judge one or act impressed by the other.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
She listened. That made her different.

---

## TAG

### INT. HIGH SCHOOL HALLWAY — DAY

Sarah walks with Manny and Butch, now easy in the group.

**SARAH**
What's Lucky's studio like?

Manny and Butch exchange a look.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
By the time she asked about Lucky's studio, she was already part of the story. She just did not know how deeply.

**FADE OUT.**

**END OF EPISODE**
