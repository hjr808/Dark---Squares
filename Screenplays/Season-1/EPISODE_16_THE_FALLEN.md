# DARK SQUARES
## Episode 16 — "THE FALLEN"

**Television Screenplay Adaptation**

---

## TEASER

**FADE IN:**

### EXT. ELLA MAE'S PORCH — MORNING

Long black lines of birds cross the city sky toward somewhere warmer. Manny watches. Butch stands beside him.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Fall came with birds in the sky. Manny watched them from Ella Mae's porch and wondered how they always knew when to leave.

**MANNY**
Birds of a feather,

Butch looks up.

**BUTCH**
They stay together.

**CUT TO TITLE: DARK SQUARES**

---

## ACT ONE

### EXT. THE ROCKS / LUCKY'S ROOM — SERIES OF DAYS

Leaves fall. The air sharpens.

— Basketball happens in whatever daylight remains after school.

— Music gets louder as closed windows hold the sound.

— Conversations move from games to money, cars, girls, and what comes next.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
The season changed everything slowly.

### EXT. THE ROCKS — EVENING

Butch counts a small roll and splits it evenly. He offers Manny his share.

**BUTCH**
Half yours.

Manny looks at it.

**MANNY**
You sure?

**BUTCH**
We partners.

Manny takes the money.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Manny and Butch had quietly become partners in more than friendship. Small street money. Errands. Connections.

---

## ACT TWO

### EXT. THE ROCKS — CONTINUOUS

Manny closes his hand around the money.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Nothing that looked huge yet. Just enough to put cash in their pockets and make them feel older than sixteen.

Manny and Butch move on through the evening.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Partners shared profit. They also shared risk. Neither boy understood that part yet.

### EXT. THE ROCKS — NIGHT

Keith moves through a crowd, rapping, joking, floating. Manny watches, protective.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Manny tried to keep him there as long as possible. He wanted his little brother to enjoy being young before the streets taught him the price of being noticed.

Keith disappears briefly into the group, then comes back laughing.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
But the world was already watching Keith. The world was watching all of them.

---

## TAG

### EXT. ELLA MAE'S PORCH — DUSK

Birds cross the darkening sky. Manny watches them go.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Fall meant leaves falling, birds leaving, innocence thinning.

Manny turns toward the house.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
The title did not mean someone had fallen yet. It meant the falling had begun.

**FADE OUT.**

**END OF EPISODE**
