# DARK SQUARES
## Episode 17 — "THE INVITATION"

**Television Screenplay Adaptation**

---

## TEASER

**FADE IN:**

### INT. HIGH SCHOOL HALLWAY — DAY

The Sweet Sixteen travels by mouth through the halls.

**STUDENT #1**
You going?

**STUDENT #2**
What you wearing?

**STUDENT #3**
I heard Lucky bringing speakers.

**STUDENT #4**
I heard everybody from the Woods coming.

**STUDENT #5**
I heard the Rocks too.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
The Sweet Sixteen was not printed on invitations. It moved by mouth.

**CUT TO TITLE: DARK SQUARES**

---

## ACT ONE

### INT. HIGH SCHOOL HALLWAY — CONTINUOUS

The invitation is repeated and enlarged: Manny's birthday. Ella Mae's house. Music in the backyard. Food and gifts in the basement. Lucky on the turntables. Uncle Candyman keeping order.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Everybody knew. The story grew every time someone told it.

Sarah looks to Butch.

**SARAH**
I never been to a party like that.

Butch smiles.

**BUTCH**
Then you gotta come.

**SARAH**
Is that an invitation?

**BUTCH**
That was the invitation.

Sarah laughs.

### EXT. THE ROCKS — LATER

Keith tells Chucky about the party. Chucky hears it as more than information.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Manny invited Chucky through Keith, or maybe Keith invited Chucky because Keith invited everybody without thinking about what it meant to include someone fully.

Chucky nods, holding onto the promise he thinks he heard.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Chucky heard it as something bigger. That mattered later.

---

## ACT TWO

### INT. ELLA MAE'S HOUSE — SATURDAY MORNING

Manny helps Ella Mae before she leaves for work. She looks around her house like she is trusting him with more than walls and furniture.

**ELLA MAE**
No drama.

Manny nods.

**ELLA MAE**
No streets in my house.

**MANNY**
I know, Ma.

Ella Mae looks to Keith.

**ELLA MAE**
And you.

Keith smiles too fast.

**KEITH**
Yes, Ma.

### EXT. ELLA MAE'S HOUSE — LATER

Uncle Candyman surveys the backyard, basement, garage, and boys hauling speakers.

**UNCLE CANDYMAN**
Y'all have fun. Just don't make me earn my paycheck tonight.

Everybody laughs.

---

## TAG

### EXT. ELLA MAE'S HOUSE — DAY

Speakers cross the yard. The house waits for night.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
No one knew how much those words would weigh before morning.

**FADE OUT.**

**END OF EPISODE**
