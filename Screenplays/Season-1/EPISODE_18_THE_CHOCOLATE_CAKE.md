# DARK SQUARES
## Episode 18 — "THE CHOCOLATE CAKE"

**Television Screenplay Adaptation**

---

## TEASER

**FADE IN:**

### EXT. / INT. ELLA MAE'S HOUSE — NIGHT

The party begins in pieces: backyard lights, speakers, Lucky testing sound beneath the open night, food downstairs in the basement.

Tables hold pans, chips, pop, gifts, and a birthday cake. The main floor is mostly off-limits except for the bathroom.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
The real party had two hearts. The basement held the food and gifts. The backyard held the music.

**CUT TO TITLE: DARK SQUARES**

---

## ACT ONE

### EXT. ELLA MAE'S BACKYARD — NIGHT

As the night grows, people drift outside and stay there. Lucky moves between West Coast and East Coast records like he is controlling the weather.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Some songs made people shout. Some made them dance. Some made them remember.

Cheerio takes the microphone first. Then Keith.

**KEITH**
I got nothing.

Keith freestyles anyway. No paper. No notebook. Just breath and rhythm. The backyard erupts.

Lucky passes the mic toward Butch. Butch laughs and pushes it away.

**BUTCH**
I sing. I don't rap.

Lucky passes it to Manny.

**MANNY**
Y'all crazy.

The party laughs and the music goes on.

---

## ACT TWO

### INT. ELLA MAE'S BASEMENT — NIGHT

Gifts arrive: a shirt, a basketball, cash folded inside cards. Then Sarah steps forward with a white box.

**SARAH**
Happy birthday.

Manny opens it: a homemade chocolate cake, chocolate icing, careful letters—HAPPY 16TH MANNY.

**MANNY**
You made this?

**SARAH**
I did.

Manny's GIRL COUSIN leans in, grinning.

**GIRL COUSIN**
Ooooo. She like you.

The basement explodes with laughter. Sarah's face warms.

**SARAH**
I just wanted him to have something personal.

**GIRL COUSIN**
Personal. Mmm-hmm.

Manny looks at Sarah, smiling.

**MANNY**
Nobody ever baked me a cake before.

Sarah's smile softens.

**SARAH**
Now somebody has.

---

## TAG

### INT. ELLA MAE'S BASEMENT / EXT. BACKYARD — NIGHT

For a moment the basement feels warmer. Then the backyard music pulls everybody outside.

The cake stays alone on the table.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
The cake stayed behind on the table like a quiet secret.

**FADE OUT.**

**END OF EPISODE**
