# DARK SQUARES
## Episode 19 — "THE BACKYARD"

**Television Screenplay Adaptation**

---

## TEASER

**FADE IN:**

### EXT. ELLA MAE'S BACKYARD — LATE NIGHT

Lucky's DJ table sits beneath the backyard light. Speakers stand near the fence. Extension cords cross the grass. Milk crates full of records rest at Lucky's feet.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
By late night, the backyard had become its own city.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
The basement was for eating. The backyard was for living.

Nutty Nate moves through the party with alcohol.

**CUT TO TITLE: DARK SQUARES**

---

## ACT ONE

### EXT. ELLA MAE'S BACKYARD — CONTINUOUS

Nutty Nate passes cups, smiling.

**NUTTY NATE**
Easy. It'll sneak up on you.

Most do not listen.

### EXT. BEHIND THE GARAGE — LATER

The basketball team tries to smoke in secret. Uncle Candyman steps around the corner.

**UNCLE CANDYMAN**
What y'all smoking?

The boys freeze. One tries to hide it.

**UNCLE CANDYMAN**
Let me see.

He studies it, shakes his head, and laughs.

**UNCLE CANDYMAN**
This some bullshit.

The boys laugh, unsure if they are safe or in trouble. Candyman pulls out his own bag.

**UNCLE CANDYMAN**
If y'all want real weed, this what you want. Fifty dollars.

They laugh until they realize he is serious. Somebody pays. Candyman walks back toward the party like nothing happened.

---

## ACT TWO

### INT. ELLA MAE'S HOUSE — LATER

People sneak into the bathroom together and get caught. They come out trying to look innocent while the hallway laughs them back outside.

**VOICE (O.S.)**
The freaks came out at night.

Everybody laughs because the party is loose enough for the truth to be funny.

### INT. ELLA MAE'S BASEMENT — LATER

Manny cleans because it is his mother's house. Sarah notices and helps stack plates, move gifts, save the rest of her chocolate cake, and clear tables.

For a few minutes they are the last two downstairs.

Butch comes down and helps too.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
It was not jealousy. It was friendship.

A quiet moment passes between Manny and Sarah, something both feel and neither touches. They laugh it off and keep cleaning.

---

## TAG

### EXT. ELLA MAE'S BACKYARD — NIGHT

The music gets louder outside. Manny, Sarah, and Butch emerge from the basement together.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Outside, the music got louder. Last Dance had begun before anyone knew that was what it would become.

**FADE OUT.**

**END OF EPISODE**
