# DARK SQUARES
## Episode 20 — "LAST DANCE"

**Television Screenplay Adaptation**

---

## TEASER

**FADE IN:**

### EXT. ELLA MAE'S BACKYARD — 11:30 P.M.

Manny watches people leave in waves: basketball team, family, cousins pulled into cars, younger kids asleep before the corner.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Around 11:30, Manny thought the party might shut down by one. He was wrong.

The people who remain want to get lit.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
By 12:35, the birthday party had become an after-party.

**CUT TO TITLE: DARK SQUARES**

---

## ACT ONE

### EXT. ELLA MAE'S BACKYARD — 12:35 A.M.

Manny climbs onto Lucky's DJ table. He reaches past hip-hop records and pulls out funk.

Lucky sees the record and laughs.

**LUCKY**
You going there?

Manny smiles.

**MANNY**
Watch.

The needle drops. The backyard explodes. People sitting jump up; people leaving turn around.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
The funk woke the yard back up and made everyone forget how late it was.

### EXT. ELLA MAE'S BACKYARD — LATER

Chucky stands apart, feeling out of place. Keith is pulled into freestyles, jokes, helping Manny, and the crowd.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
Chucky took it personal because lonely people often hear absence as rejection.

Butch checks on Chucky first because Uncle Candyman noticed. Sarah sees Butch has spoken to him and brings **MICHELLE**, a girl about Chucky's height.

**SARAH**
Michelle, this Chucky.

Michelle and Chucky begin to dance.

---

## ACT TWO

### EXT. ELLA MAE'S BACKYARD — LATER

At first Michelle dances with Chucky because Sarah sent her. Then she keeps dancing because she likes him.

Chucky brags a little about making money.

**CHUCKY**
I'ma take you shopping for gym shoes.

**CHUCKY**
Next Saturday.

Michelle gives him a kiss on the cheek. For a while, Chucky has a place, a person, and a smile that is not forced.

Someone teases. Not cruelly—party talk—but Chucky is sensitive to laughter.

A GIRL gets sick. Butch steps up.

**BUTCH**
I'll take her home safe.

Sarah trusts him. Butch leaves for a short while, then returns to find the party still alive.

### EXT. ELLA MAE'S BACKYARD — 1:30 A.M.

The food and drinks are gone. Chucky makes a bad joke about buying more if he had known. It lands too close to showing off, but Keith laughs it off. The awkwardness passes.

### EXT. ELLA MAE'S BACKYARD — 2:00 A.M.

Lucky says it is the last song. Nobody believes him.

Uncle Candyman raises one finger.

**UNCLE CANDYMAN**
One more.

The final record spins. People dance. Others clean. Manny picks up cups because Ella Mae trusted him with her house.

---

## TAG

### EXT. ELLA MAE'S BACKYARD — CONTINUOUS

For one perfect breath, the night seems saved.

The music fades.

**MANNY (V.O.)**
And the last song ended.

**FADE OUT.**

**END OF EPISODE**
