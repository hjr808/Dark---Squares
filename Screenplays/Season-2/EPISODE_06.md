# DARK SQUARES
## SEASON TWO, EPISODE 06: “THE QUIET ROOM”
**ONE-HOUR TELEVISION DRAMA — FIRST DRAFT**
**LOGLINE:** Sarah returns to public life as the Sweet 16 case resurfaces.

> Three years after Bloody Sweet 16: MANNIE is nineteen; LI KEITH is fifteen. The city remains unnamed.

# TEASER

### INT. ELLA MAE’S HOUSE – NIGHT

Sarah wakes from the assault nightmare, opens her blinds, and answers Butch: “I’m not ready. I am going.”

---
# ACT ONE

### INT. LUCKY’S STUDIO – NIGHT

Butch receives notice that Chucky’s guardian has filed a civil action over Bloody Sweet 16; Mannie and Keith are listed witnesses.

**SARAH**  
I’m here. Tell the truth.

### EXT. ROCKS STREET – NIGHT

At Lucky’s studio, Li Keith records over Mannie’s beat. Promoter Drew offers a showcase but demands the master. Lucky refuses.

**BUTCH**  
We do this without making it worse.

**END OF ACT ONE.**

---
# ACT TWO

### INT. LUCKY’S STUDIO – NIGHT

Sarah attends a trauma circle and says she wants a room where she need not explain every flinch. Butch sits across from her, following her lead.

**BUTCH**  
We do this without making it worse.

### EXT. ROCKS STREET – NIGHT

Vale, legal aid counsel, tells Butch his old Check Check messages and the party security role will be examined. Butch gives Mannie the notice.

**MANNIE**  
No more running from the work.

**END OF ACT TWO.**

---
# ACT THREE

### INT. LUCKY’S STUDIO – NIGHT

Mannie earns a conditional basketball tryout; the date conflicts with Li Keith’s showcase. He promises both, then hears how impossible that sounds.

**MANNIE**  
No more running from the work.

### EXT. ROCKS STREET – NIGHT

Sarah gives a truthful statement: Keith hurt Chucky, adults called 911, and she will not decorate tragedy for lawyers.

**LI KEITH**  
My name stays on what I make.

**END OF ACT THREE.**

---
# ACT FOUR

### INT. LUCKY’S STUDIO – NIGHT

Drew posts Li Keith’s demo without permission. Li Keith deletes it, then records a new verse about choosing where to stand.

**LI KEITH**  
My name stays on what I make.

### EXT. ROCKS STREET – NIGHT

A current photograph of Sarah at the circle comes through Ella Mae’s mail slot. Sarah says no one will make choices for her again.

**SARAH**  
I’m here. Tell the truth.

**END OF ACT FOUR.**

---
# ACT FIVE

### INT. LUCKY’S STUDIO – NIGHT

Mannie and Lucky ask neighbors for camera footage, no threats. Butch turns over every paper to Vale.

**SARAH**  
I’m here. Tell the truth.

### EXT. ROCKS STREET – NIGHT

Sarah answers a blocked call. “You should’ve stayed quiet,” says a distorted voice. Sarah whispers: “I know that voice.”

**BUTCH**  
We do this without making it worse.

**END OF ACT FIVE.**
