# DARK SQUARES
## SEASON TWO, EPISODE 08: “OPEN FILE”
**ONE-HOUR TELEVISION DRAMA — FIRST DRAFT**
**LOGLINE:** A traffic stop tests Butch’s judgment as Sarah takes ownership of the investigation.

> Three years after Bloody Sweet 16: MANNIE is nineteen; LI KEITH is fifteen. The city remains unnamed.

# TEASER

### INT. ELLA MAE’S HOUSE – NIGHT

Butch asks for counsel during the traffic stop. Vale arrives; he provides his plate photo, not rumors.

# ACT ONE
