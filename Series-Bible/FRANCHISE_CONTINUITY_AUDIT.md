# DARK SQUARES — FRANCHISE CONTINUITY AUDIT

**Audit status:** Completed production-continuity review.  
**Audit scope:** All 21 completed Season One screenplay adaptations and all available production/reference sources.  
**Season Two status at time of audit:** No completed Season Two screenplays exist. The Season Two Episode Guide and Source Map identify zero complete and zero partial source-supported Season Two episodes; consequently, this audit does not treat any future-story assertion as canon.

---

## 1. Authority, Method, and Result Key

### Authority applied

Sources were applied in the requested order. The completed Season One screenplay set is the controlling production record. The Season Two screenplay tier has no entries. The Season One Production Bible, Season Two Episode Guide, Season Two Source Map, Series Timeline, and Master Canon were used only to test the screenplay record, not to add events. No substantive standalone Character Bible, World Bible, or Continuity Log exists in the repository. Book One was used as a lower-priority verification source; no Book Two manuscript or narrative source is present.

### Result key

| Result | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **PASS** | The completed screenplay record is internally consistent or the available source clearly establishes the item. |
| **MINOR CONTINUITY ISSUE** | A terminology, presentation, or reference-level variance exists without changing an established story event. |
| **MAJOR CONTINUITY ISSUE** | A completed source contradicts a controlling established event. |
| **REQUIRES ALFRED HJR DECISION** | The available record deliberately does not establish the fact, or two non-controlling references cannot settle it. |

### Audit boundary

This document identifies issues and recommended fixes only. It does not rewrite a screenplay, assign a missing Season Two episode, resolve Chucky’s outcome, or infer a three-year transition.

## 8. Findings Requiring Attention

### MINOR CONTINUITY ISSUE — Terminology: Manny / Mannie

**Finding:** The controlling screenplay set and detailed manuscript use **Manny**, while planning references sometimes use **Mannie**. This is a naming/presentation variance, not a conflicting character or event.

**Recommended fix (do not apply in this audit):** Alfred HJR should approve a franchise-facing spelling standard. Until then, preserve **Manny** in screenplay and continuity documents because it is the controlling completed-production spelling.

### MINOR CONTINUITY ISSUE — Finale description language in lower-priority reference material

**Finding:** Some Master Canon language describes the finale as “fatal-seeming,” while the completed screenplay record and production materials restrict the fact to Chucky being **unresponsive**. The stronger source controls, so this has not contaminated an episode, but the descriptor can invite an unsupported conclusion.

**Recommended fix (do not apply in this audit):** Revise future reference language to “Chucky is unresponsive” unless Alfred HJR supplies an outcome.

### MAJOR CONTINUITY ISSUE

**None found.** No completed screenplay contradicts another controlling completed screenplay on chronology, relationship state, location, prop use, narrator placement, nursery-rhyme placement, Check Check order, or the Book One endpoint.
