## Key Information (Quick Reference)

### Logline
> A young man growing up between loyalty, ambition, and survival discovers that every choice casts a shadow—and some shadows become dark squares that change lives forever.

### Core Concept: Dark Squares
- Every choice creates a shadow (consequence)
- Shadows accumulate and have weight
- Some shadows become "dark squares" — permanent markers
- This principle is discovered through lived experience

### Primary Themes
- Moral complexity and consequence
- Loyalty vs. ambition
- Survival and adaptation
- The weight of decisions
- Urban struggle and resilience
- Generational cycles

### Established Taglines
- "Every generation receives an omen"
- "The streets, the empire, the oath"
